The Connection Project

Improving mental wellbeing by enhancing connection.

Overview

The connection project is a website which provides access to tools to improve mental wellbeing. On the basis of research by Klussman et. al. (2020), the website seeks to connect people to themselves and to others, with the aim of helping people improve their mental wellbeing.

Problem space

A 2007 study (McManus et. al. 2007) found that at any one time, 1 in 4 adults experience a mental health issue. A cross-sectional study by Salahedin and Mason (2016, p.6-7), indicated that of the young adults experiencing poor mental health, many chose not to access mental health care. Over 67% of young adults reported, financial cost as a barrier to accessing mental health care. Similarly, 81.4% respondents reported feelings of shame associated with accessing mental health care.

This made me ask

How might I improve mental wellbeing for individuals that don't usually access mental health care?
